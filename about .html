About Us

We have a passion for the vintage and classic patio sets our grandparents whiled away the hours in. We spend all of our time scouring the mid-west for the most select sets we are proud to put in our own yard. Hopefully you will find something to suit your taste. If not, we're happy you stopped by and hope to meet you in the future.

Planet Texas is a family run, veteran owned business right here in north Texas.
